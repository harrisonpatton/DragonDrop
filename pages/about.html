The Team

Adrian Vera is the titular main antagonist in the movie Dragon Ball Z: Adrian's Revenge and an emulation of him acts as the main antagonist in Dragon Ball Z: The Return of Adrian. He is the older brother of Harry and the elder son of King Peter. Like his brother, he can push his body through transformations. Also, like Harry, Adrian possesses a biomechanical incarnation, but this is gained from merging with the Big Gete Star. He also gathers energy much quicker than his brother.

Harry Belden is the self-procliamed emperor of Universe 7 and the main antagonist of the Dragon Ball series. He controlled his own imperialist army and is feared for his ruthlessness and power. He is the descendant of Wojick, the second son of King Peter, the younger brother of Adrian, and the father of Nino. Harry is considered ASL's greatest foe and arch nemesis. He has made several comebacks since his fight with ASL at Bell, including multiple invasions of cochlear implants.
